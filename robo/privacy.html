Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how PT Akshaya Mulia Asha ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"), operating the Sultan AI platform at sultanai.id, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our services.

By accessing or using Sultan AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

The Sultan AI platform is also accessible through our mobile application, Sultan Inbox (bundle identifier: com.sultanagent.inbox), available on iOS and Android. This mobile app serves as a companion client for managing customer conversations. All data handling described in this Privacy Policy applies equally to our web platform and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: business name, contact person name, email address, phone number, and business category when you register for Sultan AI.
  • Business data: product catalogs, pricing information, FAQs, and training materials you upload to configure your AI chatbot.
  • Payment information: billing details processed through our payment providers. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.
  • Support inquiries: messages, emails, and WhatsApp conversations you send to our support team.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Conversation data: messages exchanged between your customers and your Sultan AI chatbot across WhatsApp, Instagram DM, TikTok DM, and Shopee Chat.
  • Usage analytics: chatbot response times, conversation volumes, resolution rates, and feature usage patterns.
  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and referral URLs when you access the Sultan AI dashboard.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: as described in Section 10 below.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Messaging platforms: user profile information (name, profile picture, phone number) provided by WhatsApp Business API, Meta (Instagram), TikTok, and Shopee when customers initiate conversations.
  • Google services: authentication data if you sign in via Google, and analytics data from Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Sultan AI chatbot platform.
  • Train and improve your AI chatbot's responses based on your business data and conversation history.
  • Process transactions and send related billing communications.
  • Respond to your support requests and provide customer service.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve platform performance, features, and user experience.
  • Send service updates, security alerts, and administrative notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.

We do not sell your personal data or your customers' conversation data to third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure provided by Google Cloud Platform, with primary servers located in the Asia-Southeast region (Jakarta, Indonesia). Conversation data and AI training models are processed and stored using Google Firestore and Google Cloud Functions.

3.2 How We Protect Data

  • Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted.
  • Automated backup procedures ensure data durability and disaster recovery.

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Conversation logs are retained for a maximum of 24 months unless a longer retention period is required by law or requested by you. Upon account termination, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, except where retention is required for legal or legitimate business purposes.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

When you use the Sultan Inbox mobile app, certain data is stored locally on your device to enable core functionality:

  • Authentication session token — stored in platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore), encrypted at rest by the operating system. Deleted on logout.
  • Push notification device token — stored in secure storage and registered with our server to deliver real-time alerts. Unregistered from server and deleted from device on logout.
  • Selected client identifier — stored in secure storage for session continuity between app launches. Deleted on logout.
  • Login credentials (optional) — if you enable "Remember Me," your username and password are stored in secure storage. You may clear them by disabling "Remember Me" or logging out.
  • Theme preference — your light/dark mode selection is stored in local app storage and is not linked to your identity.

All secure storage data is permanently deleted when you log out of the application.

5. Device Permissions

With your permission, the Sultan Inbox app may access the following device features, only when you actively use them:

  • Photo library — to attach an existing image or video to a conversation.
  • Files / documents — to attach a document (such as a PDF or Office file) to a conversation.
  • Push notifications — to alert you about new messages.

The app does not access your photos or files in the background or without your action. It does not use the camera to capture photos and does not record audio via the microphone. You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device settings.

5.1 Push Notifications

The Sultan Inbox app requests your permission to send push notifications. If granted:

  • A device push token is generated by your device's operating system and sent to our server.
  • The token is associated with your user account and selected business client.
  • We use this token solely to deliver escalation alerts when customer conversations require human attention.
  • The token is unregistered from our server when you log out.
  • You may disable notifications at any time via your device's system settings (iOS: Settings > Notifications > Sultan Inbox).

6. Real-Time Data Connections

The Sultan Inbox app maintains encrypted WebSocket connections to deliver real-time conversation updates while the app is in the foreground. All data transmitted through these connections is encrypted via TLS. These connections are terminated when the app moves to the background or you log out.

7. Data Linked to Your Identity

The following data types are linked to your identity when you use our service:

  • Contact information (name, phone number) — displayed from customer records
  • User identifiers (session token, user ID)
  • Device identifiers (push notification token)
  • User content (customer conversation messages you access)

No data collected by Sultan Inbox is used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

9. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: when you explicitly authorize us to share data with a specific party.
  • Service providers: with trusted vendors who assist in operating our platform, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: when required by Indonesian law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with prior notice to affected users.
  • Safety and fraud prevention: to protect the rights, property, or safety of Sultan AI, our users, or the public.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and dashboard use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential cookies: maintain your session, remember authentication state, and ensure core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: understand how visitors interact with our website using Google Analytics (anonymized IP).
  • Preference cookies: remember your dashboard settings and language preferences.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use the Sultan AI dashboard. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising or behavioral tracking.

11. Your Rights

In accordance with Indonesian data protection regulations (Government Regulation No. 71 of 2019 on Electronic Systems and Transactions, and the Personal Data Protection Law / UU PDP No. 27 of 2022), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to deletion: request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to restrict processing: request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: withdraw previously given consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to object: object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at eric@sultanai.id. We will respond to your request within 14 business days.

Upon a verified request or account termination, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law. Conversation data belonging to the organization is handled according to that organization's own retention settings.
